Hi, I'm Heidi

Certified Personal Trainer·Senior Fitness Specialist·Corrective Exercise Specialist

My fitness journey started when I joined my mother for Jazzercise classes when I was 12 years old, at 5:00 am. I'm not sure if it was just because I felt special doing something alone with my mom (I was one of six kids), or if I just loved the feeling of "being alive". My very first job was working in the day care of a Jane Fonda style aerobics studio when I was 13. I got to attend classes for free and wear fun outfits so I thought it was the best job in the world.

My love for movement grew from there — gymnastics, volleyball, weight lifting, yoga, and all things gym related. The benefits I received were so remarkable that I decided I wanted to share it with others. I started out getting my yoga certification, then my group fitness certification, and finally my personal training certification. Along the way, I discovered that training older adults inspired and motivated me the most. They weren't as focused on aesthetics but rather what their bodies could do. "I was able to start the lawn mower on my own today", "I am wearing shoes I have to tie for the first time in 8 years!", "I was able to carry the dog food in the house instead of dragging it" were some of the excited testimonials I received after working with me. That's what fitness and movement should be about — living a better life.

As founder of Any Place Fit, I am a passionate specialist in senior fitness and corrective exercise. My focus is on empowering individuals to achieve their strength training goals, with a deep commitment to improving mobility, balance, and overall well-being. I work with clients to develop personalized fitness plans that enhance both function and quality of life.

I offer a convenient mobile program, bringing fitness services directly to your door or other space of your choosing. Whether you're looking to improve strength, flexibility, or correct movement and postural imbalances, I provide the support and guidance you need, right where you are.

My passion

I have a deep passion for working with older adults and people who present with other physical challenges and limitations. I believe moving our bodies is the key to living a quality life. I want to see all humans be able to live out their lives as independent and mobile as possible. Being fit and strong at any age is possible and it will transform your life!

My experience

With over 18 years experience teaching yoga, group fitness classes, and training clients across a wide range of abilities and fitness levels, I've developed a creative, adaptive approach that puts your comfort and safety first.

Who I work with

The majority of my clients are 40+, people recovering from surgery or injury, and those managing chronic conditions; but my experience is definitely not limited to that. I work with all clients across the spectrum of fitness levels, from a perspective of becoming stronger and having better mobility/flexibility for greater quality of life.

My approach

I design each workout around your specific goals, physical history, and comfort level with the emphasis being on quality of life over aesthetics. I do not, however, dismiss aesthetics altogether; but being strong, moving well, and living pain free is the priority.
